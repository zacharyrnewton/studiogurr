VCI

West Palm Beach, Florida

2019

VCI is a branding studio located in West Palm Beach, Florida. A new identity was developed to signify a move away from a photography and videography studio to graphic design and branding. A flexible logo text was developed that could adapt to any proportion in a diagonal or vertical direction. A set of custom-made tote bags were produced along with bespoke business cards made with Mohawk Keaykolour Holly paper and a white foil impression.
