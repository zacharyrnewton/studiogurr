Steadlight

Boca Raton, Florida

2019

Steadlight, originally known as Sallah Productions, came to us in need of a new brand image. We strategized a memorable and ownable name and tagline as well as redefining their visual identity. The repetition of circles in their symbol was inspired by speakers, lights, and knobs found on sound boards. The diagonal shape abstracly represents an “S” while also symbolizing the high-energy experience felt at live events.

Upon completion of the inital identity, we developed apparel and a bespoke stationery set. The business cards were screen printed with a blind deboss on Neenah Epic Black by Mama’s Sauce. The envelopes were digitaly printed on Neenah Cool Gray by Mingo Press.
