Wayport Travel

West Palm Beach, Florida

2018

Chris Wayland came to VCI in need of rebranding and repositioning his newly acquired travel agency. Originally named Oriental Express, the company received calls for Asian take-out food daily, so it was obvious that the current branding was not attracting the right customers. Although they had many Asian clients, Chris no longer wanted the company represented in this way. We started the branding process by renaming his company to Way- port which nods to Chris’s last name Wayland and also his favorite destination: Portland.
