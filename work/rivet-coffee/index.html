Rivet Coffee

Carmel, Indiana

2020

Rivet Coffee came to us in need of branding and packaging that would differentiate their brand from the dozens of other coffee roasters. After diving deep into brand discovery with Chris Dillow (Founder & Master Roaster), we developed a brand that was inspired by industry, craft, and a rich, all-American spirit. This was communicated through copy, color, illustration, typography, and even paper choice. Every detail mattered.

Over the course of the project, we completed retail, wholesale, and sample coffee bags as well as a shipping box set. We created stationery, apparel, info cards, and digital assets for web and social.
